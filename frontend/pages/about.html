🎯 Our Mission

StudyMate was born from a simple belief: every child in India — regardless of their zip code, economic background, or the quality of their local school — deserves access to world-class education.

We design our platform specifically for rural India: low bandwidth, low-end devices, multiple local languages, and curriculum aligned to NCERT/State Board standards. Everything is free, always.
